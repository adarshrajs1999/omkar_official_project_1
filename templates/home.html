TOP TOURIST HOTSPOTS NEARBY



KOZHIKODE BEACH

Kozhikode Beach offers a memorable experience that captures the essence of Kerala's coastal charm.

PALAYAM

Palayam is a vibrant commercial and business district, housing numerous shops, restaurants, banks, and commercial establishments.

MANANCHIRA SQUARE

Mananchira Square entices visitors with its serene ambiance, historical significance, and architectural splendor.


TALI TEMPLE

The Tali Temple, also known as Tali Mahadeva Temple, has a rich history dating back over a thousand years.

MUTTAI THERUVU

Muttai Theruvu, or Sweet Meat Street, is a bustling culinary and shopping destination renowned for its traditional sweets, savory snacks, and vibrant street life.

KOZHIKODE RAILWAY STATION

Kozhikode, historically known as a major trading port in India, has a long association with Indian Railways. The station has been operational for several decades and has played a vital role in enhancing connectivity across Kerala and other states.
